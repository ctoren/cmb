Step #1

How do I give Corner Market Branding admin access to my Facebook Business Page?

  1. Make sure you're logged in to your Facebook Business Page (not personal).
  2. Click "settings" at the top right of your Page.
  3. Click "page roles" in the left column.
  4. In the center, you'll see an open field to enter an entry:"Main@Cornermarketbranding.com" or "Justin Lite" (You will find a picture of our logo; If one or the other does not work).
  5. Click "Admin" to select a role from the drop-down menu.
  6. Click "save" and enter your password to confirm.

Please send your photos/logos to - Main@Cornermarketbranding.com

How do I grand access to Google My Business?

Add Owners or Managers

  1. Sign in to Google My Business.
  2. Open the location you would like to manage.>/li>
  3. Click "users" from the menu.
  4. In the top right corner, click the "invite new managers" icon.
  5. Enter the name or email address of the user you would like to add.
  6. Once you see the "manage permissions" screen, press the DONE link.
  7. Success!
